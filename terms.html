Purchases and billing

  • Auto-renewable subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.
  • You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your Apple account settings.

Managing subscriptions

To manage your subscription, visit: apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions

If you lose access after a purchase, use the in-app Restore Purchases action first.
